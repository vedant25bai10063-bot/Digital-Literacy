* STAY SAFE ONLINE *


Being careful online doesn’t require expert knowledge just some smart habits:

1. Avoid downloading apps, games, or software from random or untrusted websites
2. Think twice before clicking on unknown links or email attachments, even if they look urgent
3. Keep your phone, laptop, and antivirus updated so you stay protected from new threats
4. Use strong passwords and try not to use the same password everywhere
5. Turn on two-factor authentication wherever possible for extra security
6. Always keep a backup of your important files (like notes, projects, photos)
7. Be careful while using public Wi-Fi avoid logging into important accounts on open networks
-> UPI (Financial Safety):-
8. Always check the receiver’s name before sending money through UPI
9. Never approve unknown payment requests — remember, receiving money does NOT need approval
10.Do not share OTPs, PINs, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank


-> Report Cybercrime

If you face any online fraud or suspicious activity, don’t ignore it:

-> Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
-> Helpline Number: 1930
