*  Social Media 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts  * 


5 Do’s: - 

• Be respectful and kind while posting or commenting 
• Think before you post — once it’s online, it stays there 
• Share useful and meaningful content 
• Keep your profile clean and somewhat professional 
• Check if information is true before sharing it 

5 Don’ts: -  

• Don’t share personal details like phone number or passwords 
• Avoid posting anything offensive or disrespectful 
• Don’t spread fake news just because it looks interesting  
• Avoid getting into unnecessary online fights or arguments 
• Don’t post things that could harm your image in the future
